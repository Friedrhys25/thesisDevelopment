Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

talk2us ("we," "our," or "the App") is an AI-powered complaint and feedback management mobile application developed for Barangay San Roque, Victoria, Laguna, Philippines. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: First name, middle name, last name, suffix, email address, password, phone number, birthday, age, address, purok (barangay subdivision), and residency status.
  • Complaint Data: Complaint descriptions, incident location, purok of incident, urgency status, and evidence photos you voluntarily attach.
  • Feedback Data: Ratings (1–5 stars) and optional comments about service quality.
  • Chat Messages: Messages exchanged between residents and assigned barangay staff (tanod) regarding complaints.
  • Profile Photo: Optional avatar image uploaded from your device gallery.
  • ID Verification Photo: Optional identification image for account verification purposes.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type and push notification token (Expo Push Token) for delivering notifications.
  • Usage Data: Complaint submission timestamps, login activity, and interaction with app features.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise GPS location data.
  • We do not collect contacts, calendar data, or browsing history.
  • We do not collect financial or payment information.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics beyond Firebase basic analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create and maintain your user account, authenticate your identity, and enable login.
  • Complaint Processing: To receive, classify (using AI), assign to appropriate barangay staff, and track resolution of your complaints.
  • Communication: To facilitate real-time chat between residents and assigned barangay officers, and to send push notifications about complaint status updates.
  • Service Improvement: To generate anonymized statistics and reports for barangay officials to improve public service delivery.
  • AI Classification: Complaint text may be processed by our AI system to automatically categorize complaint type and urgency level. This processing helps ensure faster response times.
  • Spam Prevention: To detect and filter spam or off-topic submissions.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

  • Storage: Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore database), hosted in Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Firebase provides encryption at rest for stored data.
  • Authentication: Passwords are securely handled by Firebase Authentication and are never stored in plain text.
  • Access Control: Only authorized barangay personnel (tanod staff and officials) can access complaint data relevant to their assigned duties.
  • Photo Storage: Evidence photos and profile images are stored as encoded data within your account records.

4. Data Sharing

  • Barangay Staff: Complaint data (description, photos, location) is shared with assigned barangay tanod officers for complaint resolution.
  • AI Processing: Complaint text is sent to our secure backend server for AI classification. The AI service (Groq) processes text to determine complaint category and urgency. No personal identifying information is included in AI processing requests — only the complaint message text.
  • We do NOT sell your data to any third party.
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers or marketing companies.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by Philippine law, court order, or government regulation.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, and cloud infrastructure — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Expo / React Native: App framework and push notification delivery — Expo Privacy Policy
  • Groq AI: Complaint text classification and FAQ chatbot processing — Groq Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data. You can update your phone number and address directly in the App.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a portable format.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Complaint records are retained for barangay record-keeping purposes as required by local government regulations.
  • You may request account deletion by contacting us (see Section 10).

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. The App enforces age verification during registration.

9. Camera Permission

The App requests camera access solely for the purpose of capturing evidence photos when submitting complaints. Camera usage is entirely optional — you can submit complaints without attaching photos. Photos are only used for the specific complaint they are attached to.
